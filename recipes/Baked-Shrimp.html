Baked Shrimp

home

Decription

Baked shrimp is a delicious and easy-to-prepare seafood dish that can be enjoyed as a main course or appetizer. It typically consists of peeled and deveined shrimp that are seasoned with various spices and herbs, then baked in the oven until they are tender and flavorful. Baking the shrimp allows them to cook evenly and retain their natural juices, resulting in a succulent and juicy texture that is sure to please any seafood lover. There are many different ways to prepare baked shrimp, depending on personal taste preferences and the available ingredients. Some popular seasoning options include garlic, lemon, paprika, and cayenne pepper, which add a zesty and slightly spicy kick to the dish. Baked shrimp can be served with a variety of sides, such as roasted vegetables, rice, or a salad, and is a healthy and low-calorie option for those who are watching their weight or looking to add more seafood to their diet.

    Ingredients

  • 1 pound large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper(optional)
  • Salt and papper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped(for garnish)

How to Prepare

  1. Preheat the Oven to 400f(200C)
  2. In a bowl, combine the garlic, olive oil, lemon juice, paprika, cayenne pepper (if using), and salt and pepper to taste. Stir to combine.
  3. Add the shrimp to the bowl and toss to coat them evenly with the seasoning mixture.
  4. Cover a baking sheet or dish with aluminum foil and arrange the shrimp in a single layer.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 8-10 minutes, or until the shrimp are pink and opaque.
  6. Remove the shrimp from the oven and garnish with chopped parsley.
  7. Serve hot, either on their own or with a side of vegetables, rice, or pasta.
